About SLIoT

WHAT IS the SLIoT CHALLENGE?

SLIoT Challenge 2022 is an annual IoT Competition which is organized by the Computer Science and Engineering Department, University of Moratuwa and the Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with Sri Lanka Telecom PLC.

The goal of this competition is to raise awareness of the "Internet of Things" (IoT) and encourage its usage among Sri Lankans.

The organizers expect to evaluate IoT based solutions that can be developed up to industrial level standards, which address major issues in the world. Each year, a theme is announced conveying the problem scenario.

We hope to ignite imagination and creativity by providing a competitive background for the talented and curious participants. Your ideas will be evaluated from various aspects to determine its creativity, value, benefits, technologies etc. The winners will be chosen from the best ideas and the performance on the event day.

Grab this precious opportunity to showcase your tech skills. Let the best team acquire victory!

Organizers

This competition is organized by The Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the University of Moratuwa. The institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka (IESL) is the largest and the apex engineering body in the country. Established in 1956, currently, it has an active membership around 16,000. The Information Technology and Communications Engineering Sectional Committee of the IESL organizes this competition annually.

University of Moratuwa is a leading university in Sri Lanka which consists of six faculties. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering that belongs to the Faculty of Engineering is one of the two departments in Sri Lanka to offer an engineering degree in computer science.

Sri Lanka Telecom PLC is the Leading Digital Service provider and the No. 1 broadband and backbone infrastructure services provider in the country. The Company delivers cutting edge digital lifestyle services to all its customer segments. For more information, visit www.slt.lk

CHALLENGE DETAILS

--Sustainable energy is the provision of energy such that it meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs--

Industrialization of present world has significantly improved some aspects of human lifestyle, but at the cost of natural landscapes, clean water and air and at the exploitation of non-renewable energy resources. The impacts of these human activities are evident with phenomena such as climate change and global warming. The disastrous outcomes of these phenomena have affected the well-being of humans and all other ecosystems on this planet. However, if we take action now, we can lessen these effects in the future. Sustainable energy consumption is one step that we can take to secure a better world.

Providing a sufficient supply to the increasing energy demand using current mechanisms is not sustainable with the issues of environmental pollution and limited energy resources at hand. SLIoT Challenge 2022 aims to search for solutions that addresses the theme ‘Sustainable Energy Consumption’. This is your moment to be creative and envision an IoT based solution for any sector (healthcare, agriculture, transport, social, political etc.) that is affected by the non-sustainable energy crisis.

You can participate in SLIoT Challenge 2022 as teams or as individuals. The maximum size of a team should be 5 members. Teams should appoint their leaders, and the organizers of SLIoT Challenge will communicate to them directly regarding any announcements in the competition. The contestants can participate in the open category or the undergraduate category.

There will be workshops and sessions to guide the competitors to their maximum potential. Multiple rounds of competition will be held and competitors will be eliminated at each round. The final round will take place physically (details will be announced later) at a suitable venue near Colombo and the winners will be rewarded with exciting prizes.
